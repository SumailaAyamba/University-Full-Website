Contact Us

We value your inquiries, feedback, and interactions with our university community. Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, an alum, a parent, or a member of the public, we are here to assist you.

General Inquiries

If you have general questions about our academic programs, admissions process, campus facilities, or any other aspect of the university, our dedicated support team is ready to help.

Please feel free to contact our main office by phone at +1 (123) 456-7890 or send us an email at info@abuniversity.edu. Our friendly staff will be delighted to assist you with prompt and accurate information.

Admissions

For specific inquiries regarding admissions requirements, application status, or any other admission-related questions, our admissions team is here to guide you through the process.

Contact our admissions office at admissions@abuniversity.edu or call +1 (987) 654-3210 to speak with an admissions counselor directly.

Alumni Relations

We cherish our alumni community, and our alumni relations team is dedicated to maintaining strong connections with our graduates.

If you are an alum and wish to stay involved with the university or have any alumni-related inquiries, please reach out to our alumni office at alumni@abuniversity.edu.

Campus Visits

If you are interested in experiencing our campus firsthand, we warmly welcome you to visit us for a campus tour or attend one of our open house events.

Please check our Events calendar for upcoming tour dates, or contact our campus visit coordinator at visit@abuniversity.edu to schedule a visit.
