Admissions

Thank you for considering AB Science And Technology University for your education. We offer a wide range of academic programs to suit your interests and career goals. Below, you'll find important information about the admissions process, requirements, tuition fees, scholarships, and financial aid.

Application Process

Our application process is simple and easy. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Pay the application fee.
  3. Submit all required documents, including academic transcripts and letters of recommendation.
  4. Check your email for updates on the status of your application.

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Satisfactory scores on standardized tests (e.g., SAT, ACT).
  • English language proficiency for international students (TOEFL, IELTS).

Tuition Fees and Financial Aid

AB Science And Technology University is committed to providing affordable education. Our tuition fees vary depending on the program and course load. We also offer a range of scholarships and financial aid options to support eligible students in their academic journey. Please visit our Financial Aid office for more information.

Virtual Campus Tours and Open House

Can't visit our campus in person? We offer virtual campus tours to give you an immersive experience of our state-of-the-art facilities and vibrant campus life. Additionally, we host open house events where you can meet faculty, students, and staff to learn more about the university. Stay tuned to our Events calendar for upcoming tour and open house dates.
